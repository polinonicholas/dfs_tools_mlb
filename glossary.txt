sp_mu - the sum of fd_wpa for the starting pitcher, only account for handedness of the lineup.

raw_talent - the sum of fd_wps for an offense, only accounting for pitcher handedness.

mu (pitcher) - the sum of wpa for a lineup, adjusted using pps, exp_pa.

raw_mu (pitcher) - the of wpa for a lineup, non-adjusted.
